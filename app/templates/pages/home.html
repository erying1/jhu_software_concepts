Eric Rying is currently a Technical Project/Program Manager at Applied Materials in Sunnyvale, CA. He has a B.S./M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon Univiersity in Pittsburgh, PA. He also has a PhD in Electrical Engineering from North Carolina State University in Raleigh, NC where he conducted multidisciplinary work involving selective silicon epitaxy as well as wavelet networks, which are a type of feedforward neural network.


He is currently enrolled in the M.S. in Artificial Intelligence online program for Engineering Professionals at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, MD.
